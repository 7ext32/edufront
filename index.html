Welcome to Edunest

Transforming Education - Transforming Lives
Education at Edunest

EDUNEST seeks to help individuals and nations to identify and develop the knowledge, skills and values that drive better jobs and better lives, generate prosperity and promote social inclusion. It assists EDUNEST countries and partner economies in designing and managing their education and skills systems, and in implementing reforms, so that citizens can develop the knowledge, skills, attitudes, and values they need throughout their lives.
Introduction

EDUNEST is a globally recognized international organization dedicated to crafting and promoting policies that advance the ideals of prosperity, equality, opportunity, and well-being for every individual. Leveraging over six decades of expertise and knowledge, we are committed to shaping policies that pave the way for a brighter future. Our collaborative efforts with governments, policymakers, and engaged citizens are focused on establishing robust, evidence-based international standards and developing innovative solutions to address a diverse spectrum of social, economic, and environmental challenges.

Our Milestones

  • The International Early Learning and Child Well-Being Study

    IELS is the world's first assessment of early learning and child well-being, evaluating cognitive, social, and emotional development in 5-year-olds. It provides data for inclusive learning policy design globally.

  • EDUNEST Survey on Social & Emotional Skills

    EDUNEST's global survey evaluates social and emotional skills in 10- and 15-year-olds, alongside literacy and numeracy, known to enhance life outcomes. It measures 15 skills with a reliable, policy-focused framework.

  • EDUNEST Teaching & Learning International Survey

    EDUNEST's TALIS survey enhances teaching by gathering teacher data on conditions and knowledge. TALIS Starting Strong provides insights into Early Childhood Education and Care workers globally.

Frequently Asked Questions

EDUNEST offers a wide range of educational programs and services designed to cater to various needs. Our offerings include academic courses, skill development workshops, career counseling, and more. Whether you're a student looking to excel in your studies or a professional seeking to upskill, we have programs tailored for you.

Enrolling in EDUNEST programs is easy. Simply visit our website and navigate to the "Enrollment" or "Registration" section. There, you'll find detailed instructions on how to sign up for your desired program. If you encounter any difficulties or have questions during the enrollment process, don't hesitate to reach out to our dedicated enrollment team at info@edunest.org. We're here to assist you every step of the way.

EDUNEST recognizes the importance of flexibility in education. We offer a variety of programs to suit your preferences. Some of our courses are available online, allowing you to learn from the comfort of your home.
